BuildStamp Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Who We Are

BuildStamp is a construction photo-evidence app. Contact: jamiepward@googlemail.com.

Data We Process

BuildStamp is local-first. Data you create in the app is stored on your device unless you choose to share/export it.

  • Project details (name, reference, client/site/address)
  • Evidence photos and metadata (date/time, notes, location text, optional GPS coordinates)
  • Settings (company name, inspector name, report preferences, optional logo)

How Data Is Used

  • To create stamped images and PDF/CSV evidence reports
  • To organize projects and evidence inside the app
  • To complete optional Pro Pack purchase and restore flow via Apple

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your content is shared only when you explicitly export or share files.

Retention and Deletion

Your app data remains on your device until you delete it in the app or remove the app from your device.

If you have privacy questions, email: jamiepward@googlemail.com.
